ABOUT US

Supporting Entrepreneurs in Advanced and Emerging Economies

Based in Minneapolis, Minnesota USA, Zoshani is a private investment and technology transfer facilitation agency, supporting entrepreneurs in advanced and emerging economies to Zoshani (Flourish). We exist to provide creative, value-maximizing solutions for growing public and private sector investments in emerging economies. We are Advisors. We facilitate and coordinate technology transfer, finance, and expertise. Zoshani’s commitment to our clients is to continuously provide more than service, rather an unforgettable experience. Deeply rooted in the belief that we are a family, not a workforce, and the values of integrity, accountability, social responsibility, and diversity, Zoshani’s culture is a unique one that offers best-in- class advisory solutions.
